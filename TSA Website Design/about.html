ABOUT US

How Were We Founded?

Christmastime Celebrations was founded in late 2021, when the COVID pandemic had been going on for over a year & people were losing hope. We wanted to help bring hope back to the community during the holiday season, and therefore we decided to create an online event that you can participate in from the comfort of your home.

More On Our Purpose & Goals

Christmastime Celebrations is an interactive, online event for families to come & participate in. We are also using 20% of our ticket sales to raise money for doctors & other health-based organizations to help combat COVID. We hope to raise $5,000 by the end of 2022, when our next major event will be.

How much do tickets cost? +
Tickets will cost $5 each per event. This includes all mini-events in 1 day.
Do I have to go to every part of the event? +
Absolutely not! You can pick & choose which parts of the event you want to go to, based on our schedule located on our Event Info page.
How can I help you raise money for your $5,000 goal? +
You can share our website with friends & family, which is a great way to help us reach out to many people about our event as well! You can donate money to the fund as well, and we will be sharing details on that closer to the event.
When will your next event be, and when can I buy tickets? +
Our next event will be sometime in December 2022, and we will have more details in late 2022. If you want to be notified when we release this information on our website, follow this link to out RSVP Form.
