Our Purpose

Christmastime Celebrations was created with the intention of letting families experience Christmas in a safe & special way during these challenging times. We cannot lie, our original plan was to make an in-person venue for this event. However, due to COVID, our team worked hard to set up an amazing Christmas-based online event for everyone to enjoy.
