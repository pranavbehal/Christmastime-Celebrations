How Were We Founded?

Christmastime Celebrations was founded in late 2021, when the COVID pandemic had been going on for over a year & people were losing hope. We wanted to help bring hope back to the community during the holiday season, and therefore we decided to create an online event that you can participate in from the comfort of your home.

Our Purpose & Goals

Christmastime Celebrations is an interactive, online event for families to come & participate in. We are using 20% of our ticket sales to raise money for doctors & health-based organizations to help combat COVID. Our goal is to raise $5,000 by the end of 2022, when our next major event will begin.
