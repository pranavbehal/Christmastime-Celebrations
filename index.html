Explore the Joy of Christmas with Christmastime Celebrations

Our Purpose

Christmastime Celebrations was created with the intention of letting families experience Christmas in a safe & special way during these challenging times. Our original plan was to make an in-person venue for this event. However, due to COVID-19, our team worked hard to set up an amazing Christmas event online for everyone to enjoy, no matter when, where, or with who!

About Our Event

Our event will be multiple hours long with multiple mini-events! You can choose whether you want to attend all of them or go to a few for the same low price of $5! For more information, go to our event page with the button below.
